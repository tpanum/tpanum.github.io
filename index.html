Quantifying the intelligence of intelligent systems, is equally important to the act of measuring its absence.

Thomas Kobber PanumPhD Student at Aalborg University

With the surging expectations of modern intelligent systems, the demand for robustness acts as a corner stone for fulfilling these expectations. Through my research as a PhD student at Department of Electronic Systems, Aalborg University (AAU), I seek explore and expand the knowledge of robustness in machine learning methods, particularly for adversarial settings.

I obtained a M.Sc. in Machine Intelligence (thesis) and B.Sc. in Software Engineering from AAU, following some years in industry to apply machine learning for a variety of business applications. These applications covered multiple domains, such as: medical (Enversion), insurance (Ecsact, Tryg Insurance) and logistics (Ecsact).
