Academia

This section covers some the products of my time in academia. As I moved into an industrial role, I naturally do not engage full-time in research activities anymore. Most of my research have been within the field of adversarial machine learning, which I ’to-the-best-of-my-ability’ still try to follow. If you have questions or comments about my publications, send me an email.
